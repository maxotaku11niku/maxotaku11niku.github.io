About

Hello there! I'm Max, and I make stuff. Stuff you may or may not care about, but stuff nonetheless. I make art, I make music, and I make software. But it's all shit really. Artistically, I prefer to draw cute chubby anime girls, musically I prefer to make chiptunes (especially using FM synthesis), and I really do like fiddling around with emulations of old hardware. Quite the mix, hm?

I currently live on an island off the coast of Europe. A fairly big island you may have heard of. I don't really want to reveal too many personal details, so let's leave it at that.

This website is meant to show off some of the stuff I've made. It was written directly in HTML and CSS rather than being built in a 'website builder'. Definitely doing it the hard way here, to be honest. Nevertheless, at least I'm not going to fall prey to the trappings of 'modern websites'. So these pages should load pretty quickly, I hope.
